# Privacy and Personal Data Protection Policy

**Effective Date:** 12.06.2026

**🇹🇷 [Click here for the Turkish version of this text.**](https://github.com/Splash5756/KebapBot-PrivacyPolicy/blob/main/PrivacyPolicy-TR.md)

The Kebap Discord Bot Data Privacy Policy (**“KEBAP BOT”**) regulates the terms and conditions for the collection, processing, and storage of personal data obtained during the use of the services offered by the developer team, in accordance with the **Law No. 6698 on the Protection of Personal Data (“KVKK”)** and the **Discord Developer Terms of Service**.

All users on the Discord platform who are present on a server where Kebap Bot is active, or who benefit from the services of the bot (**“User”**), shall be deemed to have read and accepted this policy.

---

### 1. Processed Personal Data and Data Minimization

Only the following data, which is strictly necessary for the execution of the services, is processed by Kebap Bot:

* **Identity and Account Data:** Publicly available data such as user identification numbers (*User ID*), user profile images (*User Profile Image*), server identification numbers (*Guild ID*), role identification numbers (*Role ID*), and channel identification numbers (*Channel ID*) are stored.
* **Content Data (Message Content):** For the purpose of executing cybersecurity and automated moderation processes; message contents, URL links, and media/files attached to messages (*Attachment*) are processed instantly and stored temporarily.

---

### 2. Purposes and Legal Grounds for Processing Personal Data

The collected data is processed based on the legal grounds of **Article 5/2(c) of the KVKK** *"Provided that it is directly related to the conclusion or fulfilment of a contract"* and **Article 5/2(f) of the KVKK** *"Provided that it is mandatory for the legitimate interests of the data controller, provided that the fundamental rights and freedoms of the data subject are not harmed"* for the following purposes:

* **Cybersecurity and Fraud Prevention (Anti-Phishing/Scam):** In order to detect phishing links and malicious software that threaten the security of users, contents are processed by **Google Gemini AI tools** ([Gemini](https://gemini.google.com/) and [Gemini CLI](https://geminicli.com/)), whose analysis results are utilized by Kebap Bot for security purposes. This process is subject to Google's data retention policies.
* **Server Management:** In order to moderate support requests more efficiently and to enable users to create support requests via the ticket system; message contents, profile data, channel information, and user IDs are stored.

---

### 3. Data Retention Period and Destruction Policy

* **Temporary Data (Messages and Images):** Message texts and downloaded media files processed for phishing/fraud analysis are permanently destroyed from the Kebap Bot server immediately after the artificial intelligence analysis process is completed. This data is under no circumstances stored in the Kebap Bot database.
* **Permanent Data:** Messages written in support ticket channels opened through Kebap Bot and the publicly available information of the users who own the messages are stored for the purposes of; determining the terms agreed upon between the parties in commercial advertising and cooperation processes carried out over the server; managing potential withdrawal, refund, and objection processes; and fulfilling legal obligations that may arise before taxation and official authorities.

---

### 4. Transfer to Third Parties and Data Sharing

User data is under no circumstances used for commercial purposes, sold, or shared with advertising agencies by Kebap Bot.

For the purpose of cybersecurity analysis, message contents may be transmitted to analysis engines ([Gemini](https://gemini.google.com/) and [Gemini CLI](https://geminicli.com/)) via a secure and isolated API/CLI bridge. However, this data is secured under the privacy agreements of the service providers ([Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and [Gemini Privacy Notices](https://developers.google.com/gemini-code-assist/resources/privacy-notices)).

---

### 5. Amendments to the Policy

The Kebap Bot developer team reserves the right to **unilaterally update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice to the users**, in order to reflect changes in data processing activities or to comply with legal regulations. Any amendments shall become effective immediately upon the publication of the revised policy at this link. Continuous use of Kebap Bot services by the User constitutes acceptance of the amended privacy terms.
